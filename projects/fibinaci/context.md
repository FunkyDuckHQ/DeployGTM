# Fibinaci — Project Context

## Status
Active

## Objective
Decide whether there is a real advisory fit and, if so, what structure makes sense given that the founders are early and cash-light.

## What this project is
Fibinaci is an early startup building an automated company context / knowledge graph layer. The founders see context as the moat and models as commodities. They are early, equity-heavy, and looking for design partners plus GTM help.

## Current state
- Founder conversation happened.
- Product thesis is interesting and ambitious.
- They are very early and offered an equity-oriented path.
- They want help with design partners, GTM structure, and eventual sales buildout.

## Key facts
- The core idea is a self-serve context layer that makes AI systems more useful inside companies.
- They are aiming at companies large enough to need structure but too small for heavy Palantir-style consulting.
- They are still looking for design partners and a repeatable motion.
- The immediate need is strategic and commercial shaping, not just outbound execution.

## Decisions already made
- No commitment should happen before clearer structure and product visibility.
- Product value must be translated from founder language to buyer language.
- The right entry may be bounded advisory rather than open-ended unpaid work.

## Success looks like
- A clear yes / no on involvement.
- If yes, a defined scope and structure.
- If no, a warm but disciplined posture.
