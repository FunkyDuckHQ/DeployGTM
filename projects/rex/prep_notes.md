# Rex — Intro Call Prep Notes

*Light prep only. This is discovery, not a close. The goal is signal detection.*

---

## The only goal for this call

Leave knowing three things:
1. What is the actual opportunity (job, contract, partnership, intro)?
2. Does it fit where Matthew is trying to go?
3. What's the next step if it does?

Everything else is secondary.

---

## What to listen for

**Is this a real opportunity?**
- Do they have a clear need, a budget, and a timeline?
- Or is this a "we're exploring" conversation with no near-term decision?

**What's the role / engagement shape?**
- Full-time? Fractional? Contract? Advisory?
- Is it GTM leadership, pipeline execution, or something else?
- Early-stage build work or managing an existing team/system?

**Who is this company and what do they sell?**
- B2B or B2C?
- Product-market fit confirmed or still searching?
- Are they a potential DeployGTM client, a job opportunity, or something else entirely?

---

## Questions to ask (pick 2–3)

- "Tell me about the company — what are you building and who are you selling to?"
- "What triggered this conversation? What's the problem you're trying to solve?"
- "What does success look like in 90 days for whoever takes this role?"
- "What have you tried that hasn't worked?"
- "What does the current GTM motion look like?"

---

## Green flags (worth pursuing)

- B2B SaaS, early-stage, clear product
- GTM infrastructure gap they can articulate
- Budget exists and there's a timeline
- They have some customers — not pre-product
- The role plays to Matthew's actual strengths (building systems, not running legacy playbooks)

## Red flags (proceed with caution or decline)

- Consumer product or B2C
- Wants "more leads" without interest in infrastructure
- Equity-only with no near-term cash path and no clear product-market fit
- "We need someone to figure out our GTM" with no clarity on what they've tried
- Expects a hiring-manager dynamic when they're pre-structure

---

## After the call

Update open-loops.md with one paragraph:
- What is Rex actually (company, opportunity type)?
- Green or red signal?
- Next step or close it out?

---

*Time investment for this call: 30–45 minutes max. Show up curious, not prepared. The preparation is knowing what questions to ask, not memorizing their website.*
