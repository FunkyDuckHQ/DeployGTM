# Peregrine Space — Project Context

## Status
Active

## Objective
Turn the Peregrine work into a live DeployGTM proof point and, if possible, a paid engagement.

## What this project is
Peregrine Space is an early-stage space hardware company building optical payloads and related infrastructure. They have real traction, including a meaningful contract, but are running into a flight heritage objection as they move upmarket.

## Current state
- Founder is engaged and already acted on at least one surfaced account.
- Research and product understanding exist.
- Core GTM OS concept is mapped: signal -> scoring -> messaging -> CRM -> engagement recapture.
- Need one clean client-facing proof-point artifact that shows the value of the work.

## Key facts
- The real issue is not just targeting. It is getting qualified buyers to part with money.
- A strong angle is to target buyers and partners who can move without strict heritage requirements.
- The work can be packaged as a GTM operating system, not just account research.
- This project is useful both as potential revenue and as a case study for DeployGTM.

## Decisions already made
- Peregrine should live as its own project, separate from DeployGTM master.
- Outreach should lead with buyer problem and timing, not product feature dumping.
- The deliverable should feel like a system, not a contact list.

## Success looks like
- One polished artifact Tyler can react to immediately.
- Clear next-step ask tied to packaging or paid work.
- Reusable case-study material for DeployGTM.
