# DeployGTM — Product & Service Context

## What we do (one sentence)

We build the outbound pipeline infrastructure that turns buying signals into qualified meetings for early-stage B2B SaaS companies.

## We are not

- A lead gen agency (we build infrastructure, not lists)
- A fractional CRO (we execute, not advise)
- A Clay shop (we build the full closed-loop system, not one piece)
- A retainer for strategy decks (everything we deliver is working software and working systems)

## Engagement 1: Signal Audit

**Price:** $3,500
**Timeline:** 2 weeks
**What it is:** The diagnostic that shows them what they're missing and creates the need for the retainer.

**What they get:**
- Enriched target account list (CSV + HubSpot import ready)
- Signal report — which accounts have active buying signals and why
- 3–5 outreach message templates by persona (calibrated through Octave)
- System architecture recommendation (what their full pipeline should look like)
- BirdDog account list (already monitoring 30–50 accounts)
- Octave brain (already configured with their ICP, personas, and messaging context)

**Week 1:** Discovery + signal mapping
- 60-min intake interview (ICP, personas, value props, competitive landscape, current tools)
- Configure Octave brain
- Build 50–100 account target list using Clay + Claude
- Signal mapping: which signals indicate buying intent for their specific product
- Set up BirdDog monitoring

**Week 2:** Enrichment + deliverable
- Enrich all accounts (firmographic data, key contacts, tech stack, recent signals)
- Build pain hypotheses for each account
- Generate outreach templates (3–5 angles)
- Architecture recommendation
- Compile and deliver

**Conversion path:** 60-min walkthrough call. Show signals BirdDog caught in 2 weeks. Show what the full system looks like on retainer. Ask: "Run this yourself or want us to operate it?"

## Pricing philosophy

$3,500 is low enough to be a fast yes for a funded startup. It's positioned as a diagnostic, not a commitment. The deliverable reveals gaps that the retainer fills. BirdDog and Octave are embedded during the audit so the switching cost is already created before the retainer conversation.
