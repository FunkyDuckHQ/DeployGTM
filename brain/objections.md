# DeployGTM — Objection Handling

## "We already have a fractional CRO / GTM advisor."

**The gap:** They give you strategy. We give you infrastructure. You'll walk away with a working system, not a deck.

**Positioning:** Advisors tell you what to build. We build it. If you already have strategic direction, we're the execution layer that turns that strategy into a running pipeline system.

---

## "We're already using Clay / Apollo."

**The gap:** Having tools isn't the same as having a system. Clay and Apollo are data and enrichment layers. The missing piece is signal detection, scoring, CRM hygiene, and the closed-loop that connects them.

**Positioning:** We see this a lot. The tools are right — the orchestration is what's missing. That's exactly what we build: what triggers the enrichment, what scores the account, what fires the sequence, and how you measure what's working.

---

## "We're thinking about hiring an in-house GTM engineer."

**The gap:** Faster to deploy (no recruiting, no ramp), pre-built playbooks, and we cost less than a full-time hire until you're ready to scale.

**Positioning:** If you find someone great, that's a win — we'll hand off a system they can inherit. But recruiting takes 2–3 months minimum, ramp takes another 3. We can have a working pipeline system live in 2 weeks. You don't have to choose — use us to build it, then hire someone to run it.

---

## "We can't afford $3,500 right now."

**The flag:** If $3,500 is a real budget constraint after a Seed or Series A, it's usually a fit problem, not a price problem. Disqualify gently.

**If it's a timing issue (not a budget issue):** "Totally understand. When does the budget open up? I'd rather talk in 6 weeks when you can actually act on it than waste both our time now."

---

## "What's the guaranteed ROI?"

**The positioning:** We build systems, not guarantees. Anyone promising guaranteed reply rates or meeting counts is either lying or is about to learn an expensive lesson about their ICP definition.

**Reframe:** "I can't guarantee reply rates — no one should. What I can guarantee is that you'll walk away with a working system: a prioritized account list with active buying signals, enriched contacts, outreach sequences live, and HubSpot configured to track all of it. What you do with that system determines the ROI."

---

## "We're too early / not ready yet."

**When it's real:** If they're pre-product or pre-revenue, they're not ICP. Disqualify and wish them well.

**When it's fear:** "What would need to be true for you to feel ready? Because most of our clients felt the same way — and the Signal Audit is designed specifically for that moment. $3,500 gives you a clear picture of your best accounts and what they're signaling right now. If you're not ready after seeing that, no harm done."

---

## "We tried cold email before and it didn't work."

**Reframe:** "That's usually a signal problem, not an email problem. If you're sending cold email to accounts that aren't showing buying intent, even perfect copy won't fix the conversion rate. What we build starts with signal detection — only reaching out to companies that are actively showing indicators that they need what you have."
