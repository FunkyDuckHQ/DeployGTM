# Bad Example: Generic Vendor Copy

## Why This Fails

- It could be sent to almost anyone.
- It leads with DeployGTM instead of the buyer's situation.
- It uses banned phrases.
- It makes broad claims with no source trace.
- The CTA asks for time before earning attention.
