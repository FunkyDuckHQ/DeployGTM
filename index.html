The Problem

Revenue teams have data, tools, and activity. They often do not have a motion.

CRM records, enrichment vendors, outbound tools, call recordings, spreadsheets, and AI experiments pile up quickly. DeployGTM turns that context into reusable operating artifacts: ICP logic, signal maps, account scores, playbooks, alerts, message tests, and weekly learning loops.

What I Do

Diagnose

Map the GTM motion, systems, data sources, workflow gaps, and ICP assumptions before building.

Design

Define account logic, signal categories, scoring rules, CRM writebacks, and approval gates.

Activate

Build workflows that enrich the right accounts, route urgent opportunities, and launch controlled message tests.
